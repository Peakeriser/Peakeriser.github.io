360

The fourth single off of Charli XCX's sixth studio album, BRAT, “360,” serves as the first song and illustrator of self-empowered themes to come.

Drop down, yeah
Put the camera flash on
So stylish
Baby tee is all gone
Drop down, yeah
Lookin' like an icon
Work angles, yeah

As inferenced by the title; Charli, much like on “Von Dutch,” sees her own importance with the hyperpop and pc music community, with “360” being in direct reference to the self-surrounding lyrics.

But Charli doesn't center it to just herself, she intercontextually mentions those who have helped inspire, teach, and form the mantle of 'mother' that now falls on Charli; with individuals like Gabbriette, Julia Fox, and A.G. Cook all being mentioned throughout the song.
